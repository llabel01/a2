Join us

  • Apply
    Passionate about research? We offer grants to researchers interested in turtle / tortoise protection and conservation.
  • Educate
    Let us educate the next generation of conservationists. Come visit our headquarters in Lahaina, HI to see our turtle hatchery, enjoy one of our shows, or explore our museum. Can't come to HI? We offer "Turtle Talks" at school districts around the United States! Please contact us for more details.
  • Volunteer
    Choose any of our conservation projects to help with. From cleaning up plastic and marine debris to protesting coastal development in turtle nesting areas, we offer a plethora of ways to get involved in our organization.
